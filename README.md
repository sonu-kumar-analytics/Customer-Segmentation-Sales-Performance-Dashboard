# 📊 Customer Segmentation & Sales Performance Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce transaction data to understand customer behavior, identify high-value customers, and uncover business insights.

Using SQL, Python, and Power BI, I built an interactive dashboard that helps businesses make data-driven decisions related to customer retention, marketing strategies, and revenue growth.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Segment customers based on spending and frequency
- Identify high-value customers
- Discover revenue trends and seasonality
- Provide actionable business insights

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **SQL (MySQL)** → Data analysis and querying  
- **Python (Pandas)** → Data cleaning and preprocessing  
- **Power BI** → Data visualization and dashboard creation  

---

## 📂 Dataset
- E-commerce transaction dataset (Online Retail Data)
- Contains:
  - CustomerID
  - InvoiceNo
  - InvoiceDate
  - Quantity
  - UnitPrice
  - Country

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

### 📌 1. Sales Analysis
- Total Revenue Calculation
- Monthly Revenue Trends
- Revenue by Country

### 📌 2. Customer Analysis
- Total Customers
- Orders per Customer (Frequency)
- Average Revenue per Customer

### 📌 3. Customer Segmentation
Customers were segmented based on:
- Total Spending
- Purchase Frequency

Segments:
- 🟢 Premium Customers
- 🔵 Loyal Customers
- 🟠 Occasional Customers
- 🔴 Lost Customers

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- KPI Cards:
  - Total Revenue
  - Total Customers
  - Total Orders
  - Avg Revenue per Customer

- Visualizations:
  - Revenue by Country
  - Revenue by Product Description
  - Monthly Sales Trend
  - Customer Segmentation Distribution
  - Customer Behavior (Frequency vs Spending)

- Interactive Filters:
  - Year
  - Month
  - Country
  - Customer Segment

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- 💰 Premium customers contribute the majority of total revenue (Pareto Principle)
- 👥 Majority of customers are occasional buyers with low contribution
- 🌍 United Kingdom generates the highest revenue (market dependency)
- 📈 Sales show strong seasonality with peaks in year-end months
- 🔁 Repeat customers have higher revenue contribution than one-time buyers

## 📈 Business Impact

- Helps businesses identify and target high-value customers
- Supports retention strategies for loyal customers
- Highlights opportunities to convert occasional customers
- Assists in planning marketing campaigns based on seasonal trends
- Provides insights for geographic expansion
